Skin Health

Learn about common heart-related conditions, their symptoms, and possible treatments. For serious concerns, always consult a doctor.

Acne

Pimples or spots on the face and body, common in teens. Keeping skin clean may help.

Rashes

Red, itchy patches on the skin, often from irritation or allergy. Usually mild and temporary.

Sunburn

Skin damage from too much sun exposure. It causes redness, pain, and sometimes peeling.lotion.
