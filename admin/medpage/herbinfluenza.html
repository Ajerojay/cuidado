Common Home Remedies

Peppermint

helps clear nasal congestion and soothe headaches. Peppermint tea or steam can make breathing easier.

Elderbery

reduces flu duration and eases symptoms like cough and body pain. It can be taken as syrup or tea.

Echinacea

boosts the immune system and helps the body fight infections. It may reduce the severity and length of flu symptoms.
