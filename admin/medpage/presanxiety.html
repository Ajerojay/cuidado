Prescription Medicine

Doctor-approved medications for safe and effective treatment.

Common Prescribed Remedies

Buspirone

An anti-anxiety medicine that affects chemicals in the brain that may be unbalanced in people with anxiety. used to treat symptoms of anxiety, such as fear, tension, irritability, dizziness, pounding heartbeat, and other physical symptoms.

Xanax

Used to treat anxiety disorders and anxiety caused by depression. Xanax is also used to treat panic disorders with or without a fear of places and situations that might cause panic, helplessness, or embarrassment (agoraphobia).

Escitalopram

 An antidepressant belonging to a group of drugs called selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs). It affects chemicals in the brain that may be unbalanced in people with depression or anxiety. Used to treat major depressive disorder in adults and adolescents at least 12 years old.

Check with your doctor before changing diet, activity, or medication.
