Herbal Medicine

Herbal remedies supported by traditional use and modern research

Common Herbal Remedies

Ginger

Has anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties that calm the intestines. Reduces stomach cramps and discomfort.

Guava Leaves

Guava leaves have a long history of use in traditional medicine in various cultures for treating conditions like diarrhea, coughs, and digestive issues.

Turmeric

This herb turmeric is an appetizer, digestive stimulant, treats gastrointestinal system, and normalizes metabolism. It improves the entire body also normalizes blood pressure.

Natural products can still cause side effects or interactions. Talk to your healthcare team.
