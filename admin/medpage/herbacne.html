Common Home Remedies

Ginkgo Biloba

Improves blood flow to the brain. Acts as an antioxidant that protects nerve cells and may regulate Acne activity.

Bacopa Monnieri

is a medicinal herb traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to improve memory, learning, and concentration.

Sage (Salvia officinalis)

has traditional and studied uses for pain relief due to its anti-inflammatory and antinociceptive (pain-reducing) properties
