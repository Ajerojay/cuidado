Common Herbal Remedies

Lavender

Lavender reduces stress and helps calm the nervous system. Its scent or tea form can relieve tension and promote relaxation.

Passionflower

Used in traditional medicine to treat stress, anxiety, panic attacks and insomnia, passionflower has been shown in studies to actually treat anxiety remarkably well..

Valerian

one of the very popular night-time remedies for anxiety. It contains some mild tranquilizing properties that will almost guarantee you'll get a good night sleep, less the dreaded morning hangover feeling that you sometimes get with pharmaceutical sleeping pills.

Check with your doctor before changing diet, activity, or medication.
