Common Home Remedies

Stay Hydrated

Diarrhea causes a deficit of fluids, including water. This causes the body to lose electrolytes such as sodium and chloride. Restoring fluids is vital to support recovery. Otherwise, a person may experience Dehydration

Taking probiotics

Probiotics are microorganisms that can benefit the digestive system. They may support the workings of the gut and help people fight off infection.

Rest and Recovery

Diarrhea can make you feel weak and fatigued. If possible, take a few days off to rest, eat nutritious foods, and relax with a warm compress on your abdomen. This can significantly improve your recovery.

Check with your doctor before changing diet, activity, or medication.
