Common Home Remedies

Stay Hydrated & Maintain Good Nutrition

Dehydration and lack of electrolytes affect nerve signaling. Drink plenty of water, and include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains to keep your nervous system functioning properly.

Reduce Stress and Get Enough Sleep

Chronic stress increases cortisol, which can damage brain cells and affect neurotransmitters like acetylcholine.

Exercise Regularly

Light physical activity like walking, stretching, or yoga

Check with your doctor before changing diet, activity, or medication.
