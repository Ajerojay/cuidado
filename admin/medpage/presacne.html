Prescription Medicine

Doctor-approved medications for safe and effective treatment.

Common Prescribed Medicines

Aricept

refers to experiencing aches, such as body pain or other discomforts, when taking Aricept (donepezil) a medication for dementia.

Exelon

Exelon blocks the enzyme acetylcholinesterase, which normally breaks down acetylcholine (a brain chemical needed for memory and learning).

Razadyne

Razadyne inhibits acetylcholinesterase to increase acetylcholine levels.

Consult your physician before taking any medication.
