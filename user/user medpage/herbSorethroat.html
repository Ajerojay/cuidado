Common Herbal Remedies

Apple cider vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural health tonic. t has been a staple in folk medicine remedies for centuries. Its main active ingredient, acetic acid, helps fight bacteria.

Marshmallow root

Since ancient times, people have used extracts from the marshmallow plant Althaea officinalis to treat sore throats and other conditions.

Turmeric

This herb turmeric is an appetizer, digestive stimulant, treats gastrointestinal system, and normalizes metabolism. It improves the entire body also normalizes blood pressure.
