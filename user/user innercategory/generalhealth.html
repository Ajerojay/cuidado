General Health

Learn about common heart-related conditions, their symptoms, and possible treatments. For serious concerns, always consult a doctor.

Influenza (Flu)

A contagious viral infection affecting the respiratory system, causing fever, body aches, fatigue, and cough.

Migraine

A recurring, intense headache often accompanied by nausea or sensitivity to light and sound.

Urinary Tract Infection

A mild infection of the urinary system causing burning sensation and frequent urination.
